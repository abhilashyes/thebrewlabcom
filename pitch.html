Insight
    // 01 · the insight

    Coffee is a daily habit. Most cafés treat it like a transaction.

    People don't switch coffee shops for taste alone — they stay for the place, the people, and being remembered. The cup gets them in the door; belonging keeps them coming back five times a week.

    // 02 · the concept

    The two-minute ritual, engineered.

    • › Exceptional coffee, habit pricing. A cup worth the walk, made to repeat.
    • › Made to go. Order ahead, skip the queue, out in a minute.
    • › Built for regulars. A 5-tier loyalty ladder and a community that shows up.
    // 03 · why now

    The moment is right.

    • › Mobile order-ahead is now the expected default.
    • › A generation that wants third places, not just caffeine.
    • › Loyalty + app data make retention measurable and ownable.
